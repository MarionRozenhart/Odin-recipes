Description

Lasagna is my number one favorite meal to prep on a dark winter day. Comforting and satisfying

You can make this meal al for yourself or for your lover. It might serve you well as a love porion.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Meat
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Working steps

  1. Start by chopping all vegies.
  2. Throw everyting in you cooking pot.
  3. Heat up the mixture and stir well.
  4. Take everyting out of the pot and put it in an oven tray.
  5. Put the tray in the oven for 25 minutes.

Enjoy!
